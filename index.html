HOW TO PLAY MULTIPLAYER TETRIS

Controls (Both Players)

  • ← → Move piece left/right
  • ↓ Soft drop (move down)
  • ↑ Rotate piece
  • SPACE Hard drop
  • SHIFT Hold piece (store for later use)
  • P Pause game

Hold Feature

Press SHIFT (or the HOLD button on mobile) to store your current piece for later use. You can only use the hold feature once per piece placement.
