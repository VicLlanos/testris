HOW TO PLAY TETRIS

  • ← → Move piece left/right
  • ↓ Soft drop (move down)
  • ROTATE Spin piece clockwise
  • DROP Instant hard drop
  • HOLD Store piece for later use
  • PAUSE Pause the game

Complete lines to score points. The game speeds up as you level up!
