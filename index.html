BATTLE TETRIS: HOW TO PLAY

Controls (Both Players)

  • ← → Move piece left/right
  • ↓ Soft drop (move down)
  • ↑ Rotate piece
  • SPACE Hard drop
  • SHIFT Hold piece (store for later use)
  • P Pause game

Battle System

  • Attack Power: Clearing lines builds attack power
  • Combos: Clear lines quickly to increase combo multiplier
  • Attacks: When attack power reaches 100%, send garbage to opponent
  • Garbage Lines: Attack your opponent with incomplete lines
  • Defense: Clear garbage lines to survive

Tip: Chain combos to send stronger attacks and overwhelm your opponent!
